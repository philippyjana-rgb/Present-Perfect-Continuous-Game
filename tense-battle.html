Simple or Continuous?

A 1-on-1 game for pairs

How to play

This is a 1-on-1 game for two players on one device.

You will take turns to read a sentence and decide: should the verb be in the Present Perfect Simple or the Present Perfect Continuous? You will play 6 rounds each — 12 rounds in total.

  • Tap the button for the tense you think is correct.
  • You get 1 point for each correct answer.
  • When you get one wrong, the game shows you why.
  • At the end you'll see who won and which questions you got wrong.

Pass the device between you each round. The colour at the top of the screen tells you whose turn it is.
