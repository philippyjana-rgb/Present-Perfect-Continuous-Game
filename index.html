Long-time or short-time?

A verb-sorting game for pairs

How to play

Work in pairs or small groups. You will see 15 verbs in the box at the top of the screen. Some of these verbs describe long, ongoing actions (like swimming or studying). Others describe short, single moments (like dropping or stopping).

Your job is to drag each verb into the correct column:

  • Long-time verbs — actions that naturally take a long time
  • Short-time verbs — actions that happen in a single, quick moment

Talk about each verb together before you drop it. Don't rush! When you have placed all 15 verbs, click Done to see your score.
